Biography
------
I am currently a Graduate student at  <a href="https://mima.sdu.edu.cn">MIMA</a> Lab, Shandong University, advised by Prof. <a href="https://faculty.sdu.edu.cn/luoxin/zh_CN/index.htm">Xin Luo</a>, co-advised by Prof. <a href="https://faculty.sdu.edu.cn/xuxinshun/zh_CN/index.htm">Xin-Shun Xu</a>. Before that, I received my bachelorâ€™s degree in  School of Information Science and Engineering, University of Jinan, Jinan, Shandong, China, in 2022, supervised by Associate Professor <a href ="http://2021.yzadm.ujn.edu.cn/Page/Dsxx/ssds_data/ssds_id/a8ba7dfc-7b89-18f8-af5c-5c4705349712/status/1.html">Peng Wu</a> and Professor [Xiuyang Zhao](https://ujnview.github.io/).
