Biography   [back top]

I am currently a Graduate student at MIMA Lab, Shandong University, advised by Assistant Professor Xin Luo, co-advised by Professor Xin-Shun Xu. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree in School of Information Science and Engineering, University of Jinan, Jinan, Shandong, China, in 2022, supervised by Associate Professor Peng Wu, Jie Su, and Professor Xiuyang Zhao. My current research topics are Information Retrieval and deep learning, and I am mainly focused on multi-modal retrieval, online learning, incremental learning, and federated learning.
  • 09/2022 -- Now: M.S. Candidates in Artificial Intelligence, MIMA Lab, Shandong University, Jinan, China
  • 09/2018 -- 06/2022: B.S. in Network Engineering, University of Jinan, Jinan, China
