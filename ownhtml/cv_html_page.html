Biography   [back top]

Welcome! I am currently in my final year as a Graduate student majoring in Artificial Intelligence at MIMA Lab, Shandong University, advised by Associate Professor Xin Luo, co-advised by Professor Xin-Shun Xu. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree in School of Information Science and Engineering, University of Jinan, Jinan, Shandong, China, in 2022, supervised by Associate Professor Peng Wu, Jie Su, and Professor Xiuyang Zhao. I deeply appreciate the dedicated guidance and support.

My current research topics are Machine Learning and Information Retrieval. I mainly focus on learning to hash, especially online hashing and image/cross-modal retrieval. Besides, I am also open and willing to explore other tasks, e.g., vision-language cross-modal learning, generative information retrieval, incremental learning, prompt learning, and federated learning.

If you are interested in my topics, please do not hesitate to reach out.
